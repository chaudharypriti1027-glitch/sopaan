Explain the difference between fiscal and monetary policy
Fiscal policy is run by the government via taxes & spending. Monetary policy is run by the RBI via interest rates & money supply. Fiscal targets growth/welfare; monetary targets inflation & liquidity.
Save to notes5 practice Qs
Give me a quick trick to remember it
Think “Fiscal = Finance Ministry” (both start F) and “Monetary = RBI”. Budget words → fiscal. Repo / inflation words → monetary.

Quick shortcut for LCM of 3 numbers?
Quantby Priya · 2h
3 answers
✓ MENTOR · ACCEPTED
Take LCM of first two, then LCM of that result with the third. For 4, 6, 8 → LCM(4,6)=12, LCM(12,8)=24.
You can also use prime factorisation — take the highest power of each prime across all three.
